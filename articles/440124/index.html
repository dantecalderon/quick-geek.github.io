In order not to become attached to the monthly cycles, after several iterations, we came to the opportunity at any time to give feedback to the employee in Google-form, which looks like this :



This approach allows you to give frank feedback, not forgetting the good and thinking about the growth of a person, rather than trying to humiliate or insult him. We also wrote a script that, after saving the form, sends an email with feedback to the employee‚Äôs mailbox. You can either leave it anonymously or subscribe.

Based on the feedback, the distribution of the cash bonus is also formed. Each employee each quarter has 30 "Soviet" rubles (we call them bitcoins), which he can distribute in any quantity to all employees of the department. Those who received the most ‚Äúbitcoins‚Äù from their colleagues, according to the results of the quarter, will be rewarded with a large cash bonus. Who has less "Soviet" rubles, the premium may be more variable - from standard to large, already at the discretion of the head. Thus, employees themselves influence the amount of remuneration. This allows you to mark everyone who shows excellent personal qualities, such as mutual assistance, responsibility, activity, help, to extend them to the whole team.



The evolution of excellent feedback is a kudo card. Each employee can write such a ‚Äúpostcard‚Äù by hand to any other employee and hand it both personally and as part of a retrospective.

Work on applications: what's new?


ABBYY Mobile is developing applications for the mass users and for the corporate sector, we want you to never have to manually enter data from paper media. In our plans -

  • Realize the potential of FineScanner as a document scanner that can be used ‚Äúin the fields‚Äù, with data extraction and export to any storage.
  • Turn the Business Card Reader into a mini-CRM and a system for convenient work with contacts and export to corporate CRM.
  • Make TextGrabber a convenient tool for filling out forms and translating texts.
  • Use FlexiCapture Mobile and current SDKs as a mobile input tool in various RPA scenarios, such as client boarding or filling out a loan application from a smartphone.
  • Implement mobile SDK for image capture and data recognition for quickly building enterprise applications, for example, by ID recognition.
  • Implement several innovative projects. They are related to IoT , web technologies, augmented reality, voice assistants, capturing images in non-standard conditions and working with third-party applications.

Of course, in addition to developing applications, ABBYY Mobile developers are constantly improving intelligent information processing technologies.
