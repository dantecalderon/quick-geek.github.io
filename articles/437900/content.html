In the selection we will discuss the permitted wearable (low-power) radio stations of the permitted range (LPD433 / PMR446).

These are inexpensive Chinese walkie-talkies such as Baofeng and Xiaomi, as well as accessories.



Greetings to all!

I would like to talk about the most popular wearable radio stations from China, which are suitable both for attacks on nature, and for team games, pokatushek, construction, simple daily tasks, successfully replacing a cell phone.



A few words about the nuances of choice.

This is the cheapest radio station from China. Of course there are more expensive, branded, powerful, for other uses, for example in the car.

But simple radios, for example, for $ 12 per pair, can always come in handy to establish communications during loading operations, during high-altitude operations, or when installing SCS.

When choosing, pay attention to the range and range of the radio. It makes sense to take two, three, four radios per team, that is, a kit. If you already purchase an existing kit, then pay attention to the range of work (LPD433 / PMR446). It also makes sense to immediately purchase a cable for setting and flashing radios, as well as accessories. For example, for Baofeng, “reinforced” antennas of the type NA-771 come in very handy.
