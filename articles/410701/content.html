Sensorwake

Chip: alarm clock, which wake up not by sound, but by smells.

Strictly speaking, there is some slyness in it. The official website states that before the device entered the market, tests were conducted, and people woke up from smells, of course, everything works.



However, it is known that a person does not distinguish smells in a dream, in particular, a study was carried out to understand whether it is possible to remove sound from fire alarms, as a result of which it turned out that a person does not react to the smell.

Sensorwake , however, is arranged slyly.



Here, at first, a soft sound signal is given, and only after some time, when a person begins to awaken, the aroma clock turns on.



By the alarm clock will have to buy special cartridges that are installed on top. Among the aromas there is the smell of bread, coffee, the smell of the sea, mint and some others. The design does not have any smart features, it is not synchronized with the smartphone, and the settings are made on the case.



The design is designed to make the process of awakening easier, more comfortable, without stress and negative emotions.

Matrix PowerWatch

Chip: watches that never need to be charged

Matrix PowerWatch - watches that have made a fair amount of noise due to the only feature - they never have to be charged, because they are charged from the heat of the human body.



Watches are positioned as smart, however, it would be more correct to say that we have a watch tracker in front of us: there are no smart functions in the first version of the watch. They are controlled by physical buttons.



The gadget stores activity statistics in the application.



The first acquaintance is especially impressive when you start looking for something like a docking station or a charging cable with the old memory.



Another feature that the developers say is a more accurate calculation of calories expended on the basis of human energy.
