  5. IPTV is a technology when the TV is connected to an IPTV set-top. Digital television technology in data networks using the IP protocol.

What happens and why


The federal program allowed the construction of tens of thousands of repeaters, which should bring digital television "to every home." But why not leave analog television? The fact is that in January the Government of the Russian Federation will stop financing the spread of the traditional analog signal. This will automatically turn off old TVs that do not have a special digital tuner.

The reasons for this are many. First, unlike analog, digital television does not require a wide range of terrestrial frequencies. This is very important because our country is large and even now many people in Russia, especially those who do not live in large cities, can receive only one or two TV channels, which are often broadcast with poor image and sound quality. The transition should provide full coverage of the official channels of almost the entire territory of the country.



In addition, the number of channels that will be shown for free will increase. Today it is two sets of round-the-clock digital television channels, the so-called "multiplexes".

The first set includes TV channels:
Channel One, Russia 1, Match TV, NTV, Channel Five, Russia K, Russia 24, Carousel, OTR, TV Center And three radio stations - Radio Russia, Mayak and Vesti FM.
The second set of channels was determined on the basis of the last federal competition:
REN-TV, Spas, STS, Home, TV-3, Friday, Star, World, TNT, Muz-TV.

This transition is also made because the quality of the digital signal is much higher than the analog one. This applies to both image and sound.
