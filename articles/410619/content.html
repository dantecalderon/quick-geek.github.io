Hunt for fairing

At the moment, the price of fairing for rockets is about 5-7 million dollars. Many wonder: Why is it so expensive? It's just a piece of metal for protection. This is not entirely true; it is not just a piece of metal, although this design was made to protect the payload.

Let's start small. Each kilogram of fairing reduces the weight of the payload. Therefore, light composites with a reinforced structure with light alloys are used, usually aluminum alloys. The design resembles a honeycomb (isogrid). The weight of the Falcon 9 fairing with a diameter of 5.2 meters is about 1.9 tons.



By the choice of material also fit with special care, because the fairing must protect from the oncoming air flow, from EMI radiation, from temperature changes, from other vibrations and mechanical effects on MO. For example, Falcon Heavy passes the sound barrier at an altitude of about 10 km, and then accelerates to 7-8 sweeps (2-3km / s). At these altitudes, the air is still quite dense and there is a strong warming up of the fairing with rapid cooling in the upper layers of the atmosphere. Temperature fluctuations can be from 100C to -100C in a short period of time (about a minute). Therefore, the material must be resistant to rapid temperature changes.

Also, the fairings should be able to ventilate so that in the process of going out into space, the pressure inside the compartment must be equalized so that the fairing does not destroy the inside of ahead of time.

Another complication is that the fairings should be opened ideally so as not to damage the payload and missile stages beneath at a speed of about 3-4 km / s. That is, there must be a special mechanism for separating the fairings from the payload without damaging the payload and the stage below it. For the implementation of the opening of the fairing and for the separation of the steps as a whole, there is a separation system.
The separation system (CP) is designed for separation and removal from the trajectory of the removal of the exhaust stages, as well as for garbage structural elements that have fulfilled its functional purpose. Structural elements of the SR to act must ensure the integrity of the design of the PH and the perception of all loads. The operation of the CP elements is carried out very quickly, as a result of which sources of rapidly released energy (explosives, pressure accumulators, springs, etc.) are on board. After the operation of discontinuous elements, a complex dynamics of relative
movement. The structure of CP includes units, components and mechanisms that provide:
- reliable connection of separable parts and breaking of power connections,
- breeding of the separated parts.
The first group includes pyrozamky, pyrobolts pnevmozamki, mechanical locks, shell elements with linear detonating charges. Cultivation is provided by pushers (pneumatic, pyro, spring), nozzles for pressurized gas, brake solid propellants, engine thrust.
