Wanhao Duplicator 10 3D Printer Review

Hello! With you Top 3D Shop and today we will talk about the budget FDM-printer Wanhao Duplicator 10 , abbreviated - Wanhao D10.

Wanhao Duplicator 10 (D10)



Specifications


  • Printing technology: FDM
  • Number of extruders: 1
  • Layer thickness: from 50 microns
  • Positioning accuracy: X / Y - 12 microns, Z - 2.5 microns
  • Printing area: 116x116x125 mm
  • Print speed: up to 80 mm / s
  • Plastic Type: PLA
  • Thread Diameter: 1.75 mm
  • Interfaces: USB, SD
  • OS: Windows, Mac OS, Linux
  • Dimensions, weight: 385x435x400 mm, 4 kg
  • Price: 28,588 rubles (price may vary, current on the printer page )

What is interesting D10




Wanhao Duplicator 10 is a simple and affordable 3D printer for home use. You will not find here any innovations or functional excesses. And this is his forte. Potential users of a 3D printer are children, students, and students. Thanks to its ease of use and maintenance, Duplicator 10 can be a great tool for those taking their first steps in 3D printing.



Wanhao D10 comes fully assembled and ready to go. The printer looks like a toy, but the first impression is deceptive. An all-metal construction is hidden under the plastic shell - this solution usually guarantees high-quality, stable printing.



Wanhao engineers tried to hide all the moving elements inside the case. Maximum closed and the nozzle is a good protection against accidental burns when touched.



If necessary, the standard nozzle with a diameter of 0.4 millimeter can be easily replaced. The whole process of removal and installation takes a few minutes.



Before work, it is recommended to calibrate the table. The procedure is performed in the standard way for budget devices ‚Äî manually, using a sheet of paper and screws.



To prepare a 3D model, the developer suggests using a special version of the Cura slicer - Cura Wanhao Edition.



You can transfer the finished g-code to the printer using the SD card, the slot is placed on the front panel.



Another nice and practical feature of the Wanhao D10 is a color touchscreen display with a large diagonal. Key menu items are provided with clear icons, and large, readable font is used for inscriptions.



The Wanhao Duplicator 10 desktop is not heated ‚Äî the 3D printer only works with PLA. The finished part is removed very simply: the table is equipped with a removable plate on the magnets. On its surface is a relief pattern in the form of honeycombs. This solution provides both good adhesion and easy removal of the finished prints.



The printer itself is compact - the print area is also small. Such a device is suitable for small objects that require quality performance. The resolution of 10 products printed on Duplicator will suit not only beginners, but also experienced users.

Wanhao D10 is different from other devices of the Duplicator line. The design and construction of this model hints at the simplicity of operation and maintenance, the rapid mastery of the skills of 3D printing, the ability to safely use the printer both at home and in educational institutions.
