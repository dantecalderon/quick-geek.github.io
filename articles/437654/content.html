Not so long ago, photopolymer printing was expensive and available only to professionals.
Now the cost of equipment has fallen sharply due to well-known startups and mass-produced desktop (office) 3D printers from China.



I bring to your attention three popular models for home use (prices from $ 229).

What is a 3D printer, I am sure, no need to explain. Already, many have seen FDM printing or may have used additive technologies. But SLA (stereolithographic printing) still remained the prerogative of large firms, clinics, and so on. Cheaper prices - the use of a special resin with ultraviolet light curing - simple LEDs and LED arrays made this type of printing available. SLA UV printers can now be used at home. The main costs are for consumables (photopolymer resin), since filling the bath requires a decent amount of material: 100 ml or more at a time. The cost of a photopolymer starts from 4 thousand rubles per liter and above.
