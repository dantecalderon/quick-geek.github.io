A smartphone that you never buy is available for pre-order for $ 1300


If you have an interest in all sorts of crazy things, like the Meizu Zero smartphone, which has no port or button, then you can pre-order it on Indiegogo for $ 1299. Supplies are limited, as the company now sells only 100 units of devices.

Meizu Zero at first glance looks like any modern phone, but if you turn it in your hands, you will notice that it has absolutely no ports or buttons.

The volume button has been replaced with a touch panel. A fingerprint sensor is built into the display. Wireless charging is the only way to charge a device. And if you are thinking about inserting your SIM card into your phone, then, alas, there is no SIM card slot either (your operator must support eSIM cards).


There is also no speaker grill. Meizu uses the screen as a speaker, sending vibrations through the display.

It is unclear whether this is just a giant joke or a real product, but this is undoubtedly an interesting experiment. For $ 1299, you get a phone with a 5.99-inch AMOLED display and a Snapdragon 845 processor. The company expects to begin shipping devices in April of this year.
