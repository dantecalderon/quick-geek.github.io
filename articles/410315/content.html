Montblanc


Montblanc's clever writing accessories are designed with Wacom technology: the kit includes the Montblanc pen itself, a branded leather case, which is already expensive. The lowest price for pens of this brand is from 17,000 rubles.

A tablet is built into the case, which recognizes everything you are writing on top of, and then sends the data to the proprietary application. Pen and paper are the most common here, but the cost is currently from 45,900 rubles .



A similar idea has already been implemented by Wacom itself, and was also (not very successfully) replicated on crowdfunding sites - something very similar called IDNBook ended a few hours ago on Kickstarter.
