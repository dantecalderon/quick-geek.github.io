Payphones of Russia want to supplement the Ministry of Emergency Situations


A payphone in the village of Mitinskaya (Dmitrovskoye rural settlement of the Shatura region). Photo: Novik

By order of the President of the Russian Federation of June 7, 2018 No. Pr-969, the Ministry of Communications prepared a draft law on equipping pay phones of the Russian Federation with special emergency notifications. On February 18, 2019, the draft law on amending the Law on Communication was published on the Federal portal of draft regulatory legal acts.

The bottom line is that almost no one uses payphones today, and they are being allocated very large sums of money from the so-called ‚Äúuniversal service reserve‚Äù for their support.
