Ear headset




J8 LONG-CZ is a vivid illustration of miniaturization ideas. This is a tiny button phone the size of a cigarette lighter.





Pressing the buttons is inconvenient, agree, but still quite possible. You can also press the back end of a pencil or pen, if your fingers are too thick.



Using the phone clip, you can hang it right in your ear, like a headset. There is Bluetooth.



Also in J8 there is a built-in voice masking function (voice changer). Able to play MP3.

Dimensions: 68 x 23 x 11 mm.
Weight: 18 gr.
Battery capacity: 280 mAh (talk time up to 5 hours)
RAM: 16 MB.
Memory for data storage: 32 MB.
Display: TFT, 480 x 230

The BM50 implements the same idea: a miniature push-button telephone that can be hung in your ear as a headset. His characteristics are worse, but the price is slightly lower.



Dimensions: 74 x 23 x 11 mm.
Weight: 28 gr.
Battery capacity: 300 mAh (standby time up to 120 hours)
RAM: 16 MB.
Memory for data storage: 32 MB.
Display: TFT, 60 x 80

I see through you!


Changhong H2 smartphone is equipped with a curious function: it can irradiate objects - organic matter, drugs - radiation in the near-infrared range. Reflected waves are captured by the sensor, data is sent to the cloud service, where they are analyzed, and the user receives the answer: how fresh is the food in front of him, is it counterfeit medicine? Changhong H2 can even be used to determine the proportion of subcutaneous fat on different secluded parts of your body. A molecular analyzer smartphone should go on sale this year.
