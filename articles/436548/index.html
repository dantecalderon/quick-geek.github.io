The training program includes the following modules:

Game design projects in virtual reality


  • Introduction to the virtual reality market.
  • Target audience of VR applications.
  • Features game design games on different platforms.
  • Psychological perception of virtual reality by users.
  • Time limits for a gaming session in VR.
  • Examples of bad and good game mechanics as applied to VR.
  • How to beat the entrance and exit of a player from virtual reality.
  • Adaptation of famous game mechanics under VR.
  • Formation of game genres under VR.
  • Features of the implementation of various game mechanics in VR: shooting, magic, movement, interaction with the environment, traffic management, research, flights, PvP and more.
  • Feedback to controllers in VR.
  • Monetization of VR applications.

Practice of creating a VR game on Unreal Engine 4


  • The fundamentals of developing projects in virtual reality on the Unreal Engine 4.
  • Creating a project.
  • Work with primitives and light.
  • The basics of blueprint.
  • Creation and appointment of materials.
  • Creating an interface.
  • Sound.

Managing the development of a VR project. Introductory UX Design Principles for Virtual Reality


  • The value of UX-design for virtual reality.
  • Features of the development of UX-design for VR.
  • Methodology of developing UX-design for projects in virtual reality.
  • Technological solutions when working with projects in virtual reality with full immersion.

Virtual reality project marketing


  • Release work with the VR-project.
  • Features launch a VR project with and without publishing.
  • Marketing and PR: positioning, promotion strategy, marketing plan, marketing materials, analytics, optimization of marketing activities.
  • Preparing for launch, launch and post-activity on a VR application.
  • Promotional channels: platform tools, influencer, press, blogs, video content, Reddit, social networks, non-standard promotion methods. Including resource lists for the promotion of VR-applications, divided into categories.

How is the learning process

Each module consists of clips on the topic of the lecture of the teacher, materials for independent study, presentations and document templates that can be used in the work on your game. Training takes place completely remotely at any time of the day and in any country of the world. For training, students need only a computer that pulls the UE4, and access to the Internet. Knowledge control is carried out in the form of online testing after studying each module. Given 8 weeks to study the material. 16 lecture hours.
