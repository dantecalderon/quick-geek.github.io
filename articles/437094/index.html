We are pumping Atlassian Service Desk - the announcement of the mitap and the live broadcast

Hello! We invite to the Atlassian User Group meeting specialists for working with the Atlassian platform, its internal processes and services. We will discuss the implementation of Atlassian Service Desk, the possibilities of working with reports in it and the Riada Insight asset management plugin.


The meeting will be held January 31 at the Avito Moscow office. Sign up and invite colleagues. Under the cut - theses of speeches, links to the registration and video broadcast mitap.



Reports


"The transition to the Atlassian Service Desk", Olga Mukhametzyanova, Avito



I have been engaged in supporting Atlassian products since 2013. I try to help our teams use Atlassian tools as efficiently as possible. I study and use Python to automate processes.
The report will talk about how we ran the Atlassian Service Desk for internal projects. For the year of gradual implementation, we faced a variety of problems: from setting up chains of approvals to the loss of incoming mail calls. I'll tell you how we coped with all this and how we automated a large part of the requests. We will talk a little about how to notify users, how to work with deferred requests, about the user profile and whether it is possible to do something useful with it.



Atlassian Service Desk Reports, Olga Nikolaeva, Toolstrek



Olga began working with Atlassian in 2007, in Yandex. Since then, did not stop.


One of the important parts of the work on processing support applications is the organization of operational reporting for a quick process restructuring. I will talk about what is important to know when organizing work, what reports are and how to use them effectively.
