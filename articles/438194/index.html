What does the modern Internet look like on an old device?

For this, there are scripts , and they even work, but here the features of archiving begin to affect. When a web archive shows you a page, pictures from it can be picked up from different time eras, or even lost. If we talk about the modern web, even a ten-year-old browser is not able to display it normally. I ran the Ubuntu 8.04 OS image.
