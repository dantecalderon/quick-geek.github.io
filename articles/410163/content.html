How to use





It is used for prototyping 3D-models created in the Fusion 360 program, prior to their direct release in plastic on an injection molding machine.



A significant advantage of using 3D printing technology in the production of fittings is the ability to quickly get the finished product from the developed model.



Roughly speaking - in the morning or afternoon I drew it in the program, sent it to print in the evening, and the next day there is already a ready-made sample for further work, on which all the flaws of the developed model are visible, it is possible to immediately correct them.



3D printing allows you to check the functioning of the created models and their compliance with the conceived characteristics, size and appearance.





How to choose




The printer was chosen on the basis of reliability - a well-known, proven device in the work.

As it was done before




Before the advent of 3D printing, prototypes were created manually, and then sent to the factory for the manufacture of forms and replication.

Later, when the first 3D printers appeared in open access, prototypes were printed using FDM technology. Stereolithography is the next step.

pros




  • High accuracy
  • Convenient friendly interface
  • The system of automatic addition and mixing of the polymer when working with native resins
  • Overall reliability

Minuses




The disadvantages include expensive consumables. With continued use, cartridges are consumed quickly. It is also worth remembering that the bath is also an expendable material, it will need to be replaced after some time of intensive work.

findings




The benefit of using Form 2 is to save time and model accuracy. You can create a product and print it right away, and the tolerances stereolithograph gives far less than layer-by-layer fusion.



There is no need for manual post-processing of the surface. You do not need to order a print from a third-party organization, spend time on waiting and delivery, and this can be considerable time - this service is now in great demand.



Possession of such a 3D printer is more than justified when it comes to continuous professional use, but may be too expensive if the printer is used for a hobby.
