What is it

In this article, we share our experience - we talk about how we rented and changed the premises for our service department and warehouse.



Top 3D Shop develops and grows. At a certain point, it became clear that in the current office there is too little space for everything that we would like to see there. We decided to move the service department, service engineers and part of the warehouse to a new room, and began to look for the right one. The task was not as simple as we thought.

How to search

After studying a lot of ads on rental sites, we chose the right ones and started calling the landlords. The first calls showed that no one is following the information on the websites, and the prices are very approximate.

The first trips upset even more - most of the rooms only vaguely resembled those that were shown in the photographs. Since we are also engaged in industrial equipment, we needed a gate at least two meters wide so that we could import and export machines. Finding a room with such a gate and a rental price of no more than 500 rubles per square meter per month was not immediately possible - they were not there anywhere.

There were problems with the location. It was impossible to get to some of the premises by public transport, others were located in remote industrial zones, and some were located inside the ethnic communities inhabiting Moscow. After a month of active searches, the premises were found and there was a need to adapt it to our tasks, since inside we were waiting for bare walls.



Arrangement

What we wanted to see in the new house of our department of services: a room for processing machines, a separate room for service engineers, a room for painting equipment, a small warehouse for service and a main warehouse for equipment, and in addition a room for meals and recreation for employees.



When planning it was necessary to take into account many factors, such as: location for the loader, the maximum size of the box for delivery to the warehouse, the location of the supply and exhaust ventilation, heating, power supply.



We began by drawing on the floor the approximate location of future walls, once again checked how it meets all our requirements, transferred the planned walls to paper and began to choose a contractor.



On the intricacies of choosing a construction company - in the conclusions at the end of the article.

findings



The search for a suitable production facility in Moscow presents certain difficulties. In full growth there are questions of cost and location, to find a suitable place is not easy. Bringing the place found in working condition is even more difficult.

The main difficulty is the choice of a contractor who is ready to perform the required amount of work for adequate money. The range of prices for such work in Moscow - from 0.5 million, to 1.5 million rubles - this should be taken into account and chosen carefully, so as not to overpay, but not to hire anyone. Careful and thoughtful searches allowed us to find an acceptable option - we spent only 300,000 rubles, while receiving a decent quality of work.

The second important point: in construction - no advances, only a phased payment. At the coordination stage, several other companies fell off, they were not ready to work under such a scheme.

And the third - mandatory warranty. We chose a contractor who gives a year warranty on the work performed.

We managed to find a room, rent it and equip it for work, as soon as possible.



We wish the same to all who set themselves a similar task.
