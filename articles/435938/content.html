Top 3D Shop continues to grow: we are happy to announce the opening of a new office in Chelyabinsk by franchise.



The branch manager is Stanislav Koshlyak. Stanislav agreed to answer some of our questions.



- Why did you choose the franchise Top 3D Shop, rather than creating your own brand?

- The question was initially difficult. The franchise job was chosen because we lacked initial experience with additive technologies. Such work allowed to compensate for this gap, filling it with the knowledge of specialists already experienced in this matter. Too many subtleties need to be known in order to break through in such a promising direction and not to fill oneself with "cones". And so, the specialists from Top 3D Shop are always in touch - the mechanism of their work is already debugged and this greatly simplifies the start.



- What do you expect from cooperation with Top 3D Shop?

- The most important thing we have already received is the skills of working with equipment and processing orders. Also, as part of the franchise, Top 3D Shop provides us with the first orders, which gives a good breakthrough for the development of the branch. We plan to gain a foothold in the regional market for 3D services.



Branch Top 3D Shop in Chelyabinsk provides 3D printing services, 3D modeling, 3D scanning and service.
