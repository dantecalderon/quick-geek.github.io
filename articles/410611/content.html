In principle, the magnet is rather tenacious, but it is better to remove one of the parts as if inside.



After installation, you need to tap the device slightly twice to wake up the green signal and synchronize it with the device: you can immediately set up automatic sleep tracking by time, if you plan to fall asleep every day at about the same hour.

It should be noted that the features of the SleepDot attachment affect the result. If we compare it with the RestOn tracker, the movement metric will be different. Here, for example, the same night with two different trackers.

Nox speaker lamp

Sleepace Smart Lamps are “tools that help you fall asleep.” Nox Music - one of the range.



This elongated device at the base resembles a square, which consists of two parts: the top is light, the bottom is sound. The gadget supports control from a smartphone, as well as gesture and touch controls.

For example, when playing music, the side walls of the bottom speaker become the volume buttons: by slightly tapping on the left, you can reduce the sound by tapping on the right, respectively, to increase.



Silicone gasket provides stability on the surface of the device, so even if it is careless, already falling asleep, reach for Nox to turn it off, move it, to drop it, it is still not so easy to drop it.

Nox is powered from the network - the connector for connection is located on the back side.



Here is the USB output, probably in case you occupied the last socket under the lamp, and you need to charge the smartphone somehow. Comes with several adapters for the adapter.



The top of the lamp also has a silicone case.



The entire kit is accompanied by Russian-language instructions, as well as any questions and answers, management recommendations transferred to the application Sleepace.

You can control the lamp with gestures. This is quite convenient when, before going to bed, you can, for example, turn down the light by touching the lamp, or change it by waving a hand in front of the control button.



The control button is a touch sensor and a motion sensor; you cannot press it. With it, the lamp turns on and off, holding the button also activates the mode “Help in falling asleep” and turns on the soothing music of the waves.

Light and sound "out of sync." That is, you can turn off the light, but continue to listen to music before bedtime, the speakers work all the time while the lamp is connected to the network. The lamp is connected not only in the application, but also as an external speaker in the Bluetooth settings, so you can listen to anything you like.



For those who do not want to bother remembering commands and sweeps, although this, believe me, is not very difficult: we did it.



So, for those who do not want to bother, there is an opportunity to control the lamp through the application. There will also be available to change colors and modes, adjust the brightness, the ability to run an automatic periodic color change.



Turn on and off the light is also possible through the application. In general, the impressions of the use remained positive: an uncomplicated gadget with clear features, many of which were already known and in demand in the market before. Here, convenient control and quite good sound for a small speaker.

Sleepace Headphones

Perhaps the easiest gadget to date in the company's range.



Goals and objectives as simple and clear as possible: help to comfortably fall asleep to the music. You can use them again together with the Sleepace application, where there is a special meditative selection, or fall asleep under your favorite tracks.

From a security point of view, it can be noted here that external loud sounds will still be accessible to the ear - the headphones do not have noise cancellation. For hearing, their use is also comfortable within reasonable limits - it is still not a gag. They will not be able to hurt you either - soft headphones from the case to the speakers.



We have already spoken about the value of music for a more comfortable process of falling asleep: there are indeed a number of studies discussing sounds up to specific frequencies that affect the improvement of sleep, but in everyday life one can see that just the sound in the background is already lulling.

To fall asleep in ordinary headphones is not so comfortable and safe, including for the headphones themselves. Models made of plastic can break and generally cause discomfort due to the design features.



Therefore, soft headphones and come into vogue, and become a normal accessory that does not cause any confusion. Headphones from Sleepace - also a mask that will eliminate the impact of external signals, light, and it is better to fall asleep. This way to “get away from reality” will be appreciated by those who spend a lot of time on trips and business trips.



To make the skin feel more comfortable under the mask, a special lining is provided. However, sooner or later, the mask will still have to be washed out of hygienic considerations: there will also be no problems here, the speakers are easy to pull out.



A key feature of these speakers is that they are soft, hidden in a special fabric soft pad, which makes them invisible to the user, even during sleep. The device can also be paired with a smartphone, but sleep monitoring will be performed by the smartphone, not the headphones, for which your smartphone should not lie on the bedside table, but on the bed next to you.
