Stanislav Lem, a well-known Polish philosopher and biographer, once remarked that "The virtual reality illusion, a person who is in virtual reality, can be established only by comparison with reality."

Without arguing with the master, I will try to slightly clarify his statement. In my opinion, things are far from being so hopeless - very often to realize the fact of being in virtual reality is quite banal logic and observation, able to reveal common features between the realities you have in sensations and the virtual worlds created by people.

In this article, I tried to systematize the “inborn vices” of the worlds created by people, to search for similar patterns in the reality that is considered to be real.

  1. Limit the maximum possible speed. Without this limitation, the impact on the virtual world may be too global and new areas will not have time to download from the server. Therefore, a restriction on too fast commutation is introduced into the world - when developing, a limit on the maximum possible speed is built into the physics of the world, which is tied to one of the constants - for example, to the speed of light. The latter, at the same time, can be prescribed to ridiculously tiny. If in the world where you live, the light flies to the satellite of the planet for more than a second, I have bad news for you ....
  2. Artifacts of previous releases. No one is building a virtual world from scratch. For example, fragments of the Fallout 76 code were assigned by y Fallout 4 and Skyrim. While programming the zverozhog, the developers simply took and created the dragon model from Skyrim. Therefore, carefully examine your world - are there creatures or phenomena that are physically impossible in the final version? It’s quite simple to distinguish them from creatures or phenomena - inherent in your world - the already mentioned dragon flew and glowed with fire in Skyrim with the help of magic. And since there is no magic in Fallout 76, the flights and the emission of fire by the sergo-god are a direct indication of the artificiality of the world, since they contradict the physics and biology of that area. Of course, the dragon / zverozhog is a textbook example of a similar error made at the very dawn of the creation of virtual worlds. In search of such errors, you may have to dig deeper. In the most direct sense - in one of the modern virtual worlds, when copying from a previous geology project, along with it were copied the bones of huge animals that local residents began to find in the ground. At the same time, local science did not at all consider this phenomenon inexplicable, despite the size of animals, impossible at the current level of gravity, by several times larger than the size of all existing living beings.
  3. And again we turn to physics. All programmers know the so-called "Magic numbers." In short, this type of crutches is a numeric constant that is added to the code so that the ends meet at the ends. If for the functioning of your world it is necessary to enclose a logbook defining the essence of reality under the wheels, in the sense of introducing an amendment to some kind of "cosmological constant", congratulations: your world was created not only artificially, but also carelessly.
  4. Abundance of NPCs. According to the release of peacekeepers, the number of some virtual worlds is approaching ten billion people - which is many times more than the number of possible living users. But this is a seeming paradox - in fact, most of the population of such worlds are computer-controlled characters. How to distinguish them from the players? By stereotyped and simplified behavior. For example, NPCs are passive - they will never take quests, write comments or participate in the discussion. If in the world where you live, the number of unique commentators on the largest social news site is from 1% to 3% of the number of readers, then ... you already understand, right?

I can not say whether this behavior arose as a result of a programmer's error, or was a joke - I can only note that the dialogues between the NPCs going on foot due to high gasoline prices in the suburbs to dig into the abandoned kolkhoz fields of frozen rutabaga, how hearty and freely began to live in recent years, according to the level of absurdity, they were approaching the best creations of Eugene Ionesco and Alina Vituhnovskaya.   5. Universe as a backdrop. You've probably heard about the Fermi paradox. The one about the great silence of the cosmos. "If the cosmos is so ancient and without a border, then where are all these myriad technologically advanced races that were supposed to live in our neighborhood?" Scientists, of course, managed to come up with a dozen different solutions to the paradox — besides the simplest — what kind of life do you expect from a curtain closing the stage? With increasing distance from the focus of the creators' intention, the complexity of the artificial world must fall - for no one models the central city and the secondary quest of the secondary quest visited by one player a year with the same zeal. If in your world there is a difficult life only in the center of your universe, and the neighboring worlds are whipped up empty and boring blocks, then ...
  6. Inheritance error Virtual reality, of course, contains many undocumented implementation features. Let me explain with an example: Create a “New Bitmap” file on your desktop. It is empty and has zero size. Now we will open its properties and change the application with which we will tear off this file on notepad. From the user's point of view, the size and content of the file has not changed, it is as empty as it was. But bad luck, now it opens with a notebook. Imagine how this should puzzle the scientists of the virtual world? After all, there the properties will not inherit at all the files, but the “physical” ones - in their reality, the objects. Imagine their bewilderment when they notice, for example, that white women, whose first lovers were negros, contrary to science and logic there, give birth to black and curly babies from their white husbands, even years after that first connection? I am sure that since this contradicts all local science, an amusing situation will arise when the same scientists, formally vehemently denying such facts, will take measures in everyday life, for example, when breeding dogs, in order to avoid similar inheritance of properties.

    Margin note

This worked, naturally, not everywhere, but only in individual laboratories, subject to a number of conditions that dictated the remnants of the failed script. But these are details. The reaction of the local scientists, who staged a nearly full-fledged crusade against doctors, was quite amusing, as they quite successfully treated the flu with barbaric duck's liver diluted to infinitely small proportions.   7. 7. Ridiculous and irrational physics. Recall, for a start, the rules of epicycle:

    First: Scientists, in an attempt to pull an erroneous theory on experimental data, come up with ridiculous torus on wheels, designed to smooth out contradictions. (This name came from the Middle Ages, when mathematicians, in order to preserve the geocentric system of the world, epicycles were invented - designed to explain the illogical, in their model, wobble of the planets as they move through the sky)

    and second: The absurdity and stupidity of epicycles is noticeable only after the appearance of a new theory that eliminates them. The epicycles that invented them to medieval scholars seemed logical and beautiful abstractions.

    Now, armed with these rules, look at the physics of your world with an armed eye. What is the obvious fit of the answer to the result in an attempt to explain the operation of the reality code through the "physical properties of objects"? Do you have light is a form of matter, possessing both the properties of particles and the properties of waves? Yah? You do not see anything strange in this? Seriously? Can a zero have unit properties? Okay. And quantum entanglement? Are you afraid of the "frightening long-range"? Not? Einstein was frightened, but you were not afraid. Good. Well, and two sets of rules - physical laws, for micro and macro world, can you explain otherwise than by code optimization, which simplifies the calculation of the behavior of large physical objects? Do not see the logic in this? Oh well…
  8. The playhouses of the mind of the game character are not in the skull box - the brain, exactly like the muscles, skin and bones, is nothing more than a game convention. Thinking is too complicated a process to trust its implementation to a simulated body - it is implemented directly in the executable code and translated into the body. So the trauma of the character’s head does not directly affect this external process - just to preserve the authenticity, in the case of brain damage, the game makes corresponding changes in the code of thinking. Or not - like everything else in the game, this relationship may not work due to a failure. If in your world curious cases are known, when people whose brain was destroyed by illness and (or) accidentally piercing their heads with iron scrap, continued to live peacefully on, then ... local science will undoubtedly find a “logical” explanation, isn't it?

In the arms and legs for performance and in the brain to "emulate the process of thinking" in this bag of fat with synapses.   9. Foresight. Many NPCs have a pre-recorded fate. So in the virtual world, the advancement along the storyline is realized. After a failure, some NPCs get access to this data - they remember not only their past, but also the future. And his, and his world. When Wanga said in 1980: “Kursk will go under water, and the whole world will mourn him” - she really knew about the death of a submarine. At the same time, keep in mind that forecasts can be voiced not only by the NPS - a completely random person can also get access to the general scenario of the development of the world. In this case, the knowledge of the future will be realized through prophetic dreams and insights. In the world I was testing, for example, the future of the state was described with frightening accuracy in the cycle of cartoons by the artist Corruptor.

What they come up with this explanation is not important: the calculations will be made by “qubits”, “food warmers”, “specially trained termites” ...
  10. Variability of history. Being inside the virtual world, the player may notice the results of the work of patches that correct the story. Here you need to make a reservation - if the correction patch was really large, like a patch to remove the prohibited destructive cult from virtual worlds, then not only Easter eggs and direct references inserted by programmers will be removed, but all direct references to the cult in history and literature. In the same way, the memories of the players and the NPC, who, after cleaning, will be genuinely perplexed, will be edited - what, what kind of pyramid with an eye is depicted on their banknotes? But, in addition to large patches, there are minor ones - when only the world is subjected to correction, without affecting the players' minds. A funny example is the situation when, after a complaint from Toei Animation, I had to remove from the history of the virtual world the end of the cartoon "Around the World in 80 Days" already seen by users. As a result, a ridiculous situation has arisen - when a significant part of the population of a huge country remembers the final series of the animated series, with the cabs in London and the battle inside the huge hours, but when they try to reconsider they learn that the animated series ends in the middle of the adventure - where the heroes cross America.

    Margin note

Also, the 1996 article was deleted from Komsomolskaya Pravda, where Vanga’s prediction was given that Kursk would sink - the article was remembered by many, but scans from a million-volume newspaper that was “not preserved”.
  11. Everywhere repeating patterns. In old games, sand and grass often seemed woven from squares - having created the texture of a small fragment of the surface, the programmer, without further ado, simply repeated it many times. The human eye easily noticed all these repetitive pictures. After the generation of the surface was transferred to the algorithms, this “square-nested” design method was not put to scrap, but was adapted to the new conditions. Why write to the virtual world a unique story? Let's write a small template and fill the story with it, copying as many times as necessary, changing only the dates and names. It is easy to notice - the original world develops exponentially, so that when copying, “empty centuries” inevitably arise - periods copied from the early stages of the development of society and inserted between more developed fragments.

  12. Creating the artistic content of the fictional world is not easy - the time spent writing the original book for virtual reality is comparable to writing a book for a regular publication. That is why the original literature, created for the virtual world, such as "Lustful Argonian Maiden" - as a rule, has a ridiculously modest volume. That is why, the literature and history of the virtual world, as a rule, is simply copied from open sources, with minimal processing by scripts. Because of this, the history of virtual worlds is replete with oddities disassembled in paragraph 2. For example, in the world I have mentioned more than once without magic, in historical documents it was possible to find a stripped-down and absurd version of the fight against magic covens. It is absurd that there is no magic in their world and there has never been any magic - and hundreds of thousands of magick, in their version of history, were burned at the stake without any reason. With literature, the situation is somewhat simpler - when you copy a book into the virtual world, the genre simply changes - this is how well-known authors of best-selling documentary books in our world occupy the top of the top science fiction writers. That is why I do not exclude that my brief review of ways to find out that you are in the virtual world, will be read in the virtual worlds - the article will simply be copied by another peacemaker, along with the rest of the literature.
